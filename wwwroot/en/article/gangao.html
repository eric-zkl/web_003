Guangxi economic and social development briefing held in Hong Kong and Macao

作者 广西交通台 2018年07月20日 23:12

In celebration of the 40th anniversary of reform and opening up and the 60th anniversary of the founding of the autonomous region, a series of promotional and cultural activities were held in Hong Kong and Macao on July 17 and 19. Fan Xiaoli, member of the Standing Committee of the Autonomous Region Party committee and propaganda department, attended the briefing by the delegation of Guangxi.

At the meeting, Fan Xiaoli enthusiastically introduced Guangxi and introduced Guangxi to the guests attending the meeting. She said that in the past 60 years, eight Gui Zhuang villages have undergone tremendous changes. In order to let people from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Macao and the villagers of Gui nationality know about the new development of Zhuang Township and seek new cooperation, Guangxi, starting from the beginning of this month, has demonstrated to all walks of life in Hong Kong and Macao an open, inclusive, united and harmonious, colorful and prosperous atmosphere through a series of activities, such as radio and television program exhibition, promotion of "good books in Guangxi", Guangxi painting and calligraphy exhibition, and literary performances. New Guangxi. Fan Xiaoli said that at present, Guangxi is building a new pattern of all-round open development in accordance with the new orientation and mission entrusted by the central government. He hoped that all parties will continue to deepen their pragmatic cooperation in the fields of industry, culture, finance, economy and trade and create a better tomorrow together.

Xue Xiaofeng, Deputy Director of the Macao Joint Office of the CPPCC, Han Zaifeng, Director of the General Office of the Hong Kong Joint Office of the CPPCC, Zhu Wen, Minister of Propaganda, Culture and Sports, Liu Wei, Deputy Minister of the Macao Joint Office of the CPPCC, Zhao Heqing, Deputy Minister of the Macao Joint Office of Social Works, Standing Committee of the CPPCC, President of the Hong Kong Association for the Promotion of Culture, and Chairman of the Hong Kong Volunteer Union Tan Jinqiu, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Chairman Cai Jiazan of the Hong Kong and Guangxi Societies Association, Chairman of the Association of Macao and Guangxi Societies, and Xu Jiaying, member of the Guangxi Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as more than 400 guests from Hong Kong and Macao, industry and commerce, literature and art, media, and associations, attended two introductory meetings respectively. During her stay in Hong Kong and Macao, Fan Xiaoli and her delegation also called on the heads of the Hong Kong Joint Office, the Hong Kong SAR Government, the Macao SAR Government and relevant associations to discuss new prospects for development and build a new platform for cooperation.
