Guangxi builds a comprehensive financial reform pilot area to build ASEAN oriented financial opening door

(excerpt) source: China News Network Author: Huang Yanmei 2018-6-20 14:05:29

In January 9, 2014, Guangxi held a working conference on the comprehensive financial reform pilot area in Nanning. Since Guangxi started the comprehensive financial reform along the border in 2014, China's first ASEAN monetary service platform, the first RMB and Vietnamese Shield franchise business pilot projects have landed in Dongxing, the local people get rid of the RMB and Vietnamese Shield exchange rate "apportionment bank" pricing.

Dongxing and Vietnam's Manjie market are facing each other across the river. It is the only port city between China and ASEAN. In recent years, the city has implemented a series of "pilot-ahead" financial reform measures, and created a number of "Guangxi first" and "China first". More and more enterprises engaged in Sino-Vietnamese trade and border people have benefited substantially. In September 13, 2017, the Ninth China ASEAN financial cooperation and development forum was held in Nanning, Guangxi. Finance is the core of modern economy. In the past five years, Guangxi has accelerated the pace of financial opening up, deepened its financial cooperation with ASEAN, and made remarkable innovations in financial reform along the border, moving towards a "deep-water zone". According to the data provided by Guangxi Finance Office, by the end of April this year, the total cross-border RMB settlement in Guangxi amounted to 86.97 billion yuan, ranking first among China's eight border provinces (regions).

Since the financial and trade platform of China-ASEAN (Chongzuo) regional financial service center was commissioned in April 2017, the monthly transaction amount has exceeded 200 million yuan, which has solved the bottleneck problem of enterprise development and made the small and medium-sized enterprises save more time, energy and labor in trade. In the view of Yu Shanglin, chairman of Guangxi Yida Real Estate Investment Co., Ltd., it will be of great significance to promote foreign trade by the financial reform along the border.

Qinzhou bonded port area customs declaration reform speed up

Source: Guangxi daily (Hu Tiejun Lu Jianchang) 2018-08-03 07:11　

On July 25, Guangxi Chuangkai International Logistics Co., Ltd. completed the one-time entry of customs declaration and inspection information through the customs international trade "single window" Standard Version of customs and inspection integration declaration section, and realized the "one-time declaration", which took only 40 seconds from declaration to release. This is the first ticket customs and inspection integrated declaration mode of Qinzhou Bonded Port Area. Export declaration form. According to the unified arrangement of the General Administration of Customs, the integrated declaration of import and export goods will be implemented on August 1, and the customs declaration forms and inspection declaration forms will be merged into one customs declaration form. Nanning Customs, which has been subordinated to 15 customs sites in the region since July 25, has fully tried out the integrated declaration project business of customs inspection.

"For enterprises, this reform is very convenient. The original declaration of import and export business, inspection information, we need to separate two systems, fill in twice, now through the integration of customs and inspection declaration model, only need to prepare a set of materials, upload declaration once completed, greatly saving the workload. Huang Donghui, declarant of Guangxi Chuang Kai International Logistics Co., said excitedly after completing the first ticket export declaration.

According to the Customs, the integrated declaration items of customs and inspection are the "first shot" of the integration of customs and inspection business and the landmark reform measures of the integration of customs and inspection. After sorting out and integrating, 229 declaration items of original customs declaration and inspection declaration have been merged and streamlined to 105, and the codes of eight common items of original customs declaration and inspection, such as country (region), port and currency system, have been unified. 7 of them adopt national standard codes or establish corresponding relations with national standards. The Customs simplifies and integrates the attached documents for import declaration, merges 74 original documents for customs declaration and inspection into 10 documents, and 102 supervision documents into 64 documents.
