Don’t forget the heart, remember the mission, go on the road to poverty alleviation

来源：广西扶贫信息网 2018-07-08 11:33:00

In order to thoroughly study and implement the spirit of the 19th National Congress of the Communist Party of China and Xi Jinping's new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics, and promote the comprehensive development of the party from the strict governance of the party, in celebration of the 97th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, July 6-7, the autonomous region The Poverty Alleviation Office organized party members and cadres and representatives of the party’s activists to go to Longzhou County to carry out the practical activities of “not forgetting the original heart and keeping in mind the mission”. Member of the Party Group of the Autonomous Region Poverty Alleviation Office, Guan Chaojian, head of the discipline inspection team of the Autonomous Region Discipline Inspection Commission, and Li Qingfa, deputy director of the Guangxi Foreign Investment Poverty Project Management Center and the deputy secretary of the Party Committee of the Autonomous Region Poverty Alleviation Office attended the event.

During the event, the party members and cadres who participated in the activity first visited the Longzhou Memorial Hall. Through the historical pictures and historical materials displayed in the museum, they truly felt the great fearless spirit of the revolutionary ancestors who joined the revolution, selfless dedication, heroic fighting, and fear of sacrifice. After the visit, the teachers of the party school of Longzhou County Committee contacted the actual poverty alleviation work, and gave a vivid party lesson to all party members on how to deeply understand the spirit of the Longzhou Uprising and enhance party spirit. Subsequently, all party members and cadres face the party flag on the first floor of the memorial hall, raise their right hand, solemnly swear, revisit the oath of entering the party, and encourage party members and cadres to always bear in mind the commitment to the party, bearing in mind the responsibility and mission of the party members. Finally, all party members and cadres also went to the Longzhou Martyrs Cemetery to commemorate the revolutionary martyrs who sacrificed in the counterattack against Vietnam. They presented wreaths to the martyrs, deeply affectionate and silent, expressing their grief and reverence for the revolutionary martyrs.

Everyone unanimously stated that we must resolutely study the long-term pragmatism, perseverance, dedication and hard work of the Longzhou Uprising, cultivate the sincere feelings of the masses in the fight against poverty, strengthen the firm determination of forging entrepreneurs, and enhance the loyalty of the loyal mission. Acting as a continuation of the continuation of the quest for excellence, not forgetting the initial heart, bearing in mind the mission, and resolutely handing over a satisfactory answer to the party and the people.
