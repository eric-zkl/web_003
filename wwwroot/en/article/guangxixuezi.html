Shanghai Jiaotong University Students and Guangxi alumni gather in Nanning to talk about the maritime Silk Road.

【来源：广西新闻网】【作者：赵晖】【编辑：苏琳】 时间：2014年08月07日

Recently, the Shanghai Jiaotong University's "Maritime Silk Road" social practice group and some alumni from Guangxi gathered in Nanning and held a forum. The teachers and students of the practice group listened to the alumni's suggestions on Guangxi's active docking of the "21st century Maritime Silk Road" national strategy.

It is understood that in June 2014, the Shanghai Jiaotong University's "Maritime Silk Road" Social Practice Group, under the macro-strategic background of the national "Maritime Silk Road", went deep into Hebei, Shandong, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Yunnan and Shanghai to investigate the progress of the "Maritime Silk Road" and the local situation. The government departments, enterprises, institutions and the masses'understanding of the "Maritime Silk Road" and the implementation of the "Maritime Silk Road" strategy in the local area are analyzed. Suggestions are put forward to promote the development of the Maritime Silk Road. Guangxi, as one of the starting points of the "Maritime Silk Road", was visited by a group of practitioners and held a symposium smoothly, which provided a broad vision and rich material for the analysis of how Guangxi should dock the "Maritime Silk Road" strategy.

During the symposium, we exchanged and discussed the opportunities and challenges brought about by the "21st century Maritime Silk Road" strategy to Guangxi's economic and social development. Finally, Professor Hu Hao, the instructor of the practice group, summarized the Symposium and said that Jiaotong University teachers and students were also paying close attention to the development strategy of the 21st century Maritime Silk Road. Jiaotong University teachers and students should study the strategy of the Maritime Silk Road together with their Guangxi alumni, and actively offer suggestions.
