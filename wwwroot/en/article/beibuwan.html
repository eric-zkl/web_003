Reform of new landmark Beibu Gulf

brief introduction

Beibu Gulf Economic Zone is located at the junction of the three economic circles of South China, Southwest China and ASEAN. It is the only coastal area in the development of Western China and the only area connected by land and sea between China and ASEAN countries. Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone is located at the center of the top of Beibu Gulf, mainly including Nanning, Beihai and Qinzhou. And Fangchenggang's jurisdiction, including transportation and logistics in Yulin and Chongzuo. In January 16, 2008, the state approved the implementation of the Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone Development Plan.

The opening of the Beibu Gulf Economic Zone in 2017

Source: Guangxi daily (excerpt) 2017-12-29 15:28:25

In 2017, Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone made great efforts to deepen reform and open cooperation, and achieved remarkable results. The strategic engine and leading role of Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone in accelerating regional development were further strengthened. First, the gross domestic product will continue to lead the whole region. In the first three quarters, the GDP of the four cities in the economic zone (Nanning, Beihai, Qinzhou and Fangchenggang) reached 479.45 billion yuan, an increase of 8.6% over the same period of last year, 1.6 percentage points higher than that of the whole region. Two, fiscal revenue growth continues to lead the whole region. In the first three quarters, the four cities in the economic zone achieved a fiscal revenue of 86.69 billion yuan, an increase of 11.4% over the same period last year, 3.7 percentage points higher than that of the whole region. Three, the main economic indicators accounted for the proportion of the region gradually increased. In the first three quarters of the year, the GDP of the four cities in the economic zone accounted for 37.6% of the region, up 0.54 percentage points from the same period last year; the fiscal revenue accounted for 44.4% of the region, up 1.5 percentage points from the same period last year; the total retail sales of consumer goods accounted for 38% of the region, up 0.8 percentage points from the same period last year.

Focus on Guangxi's high quality open development

People's daily Liu Huaxin, Pang Jiping, Li Zong and Liu Jiahua (excerpt) 2018年05月21日 10 版

From April 19 to 21, 2017, General Secretary Xi Jinping visited Guangxi. He was very concerned about the opening up and development of Guangxi. He encouraged 56 million Zhuang township cadres and masses to "base themselves on their unique location, release the potential of the sea, stimulate the vitality of the river, make full use of the articles of the"border"and fully implement the strategy of opening up and driving.

Over the past year, Guangxi has made every effort to implement the requirements of General Secretary Xi Jinping, speed up the construction of an international gateway for ASEAN, create a new strategic fulcrum for the opening and development of the southwest, central and southern regions, and form an important gateway linking the maritime Silk Road and the Silk Road in the 21st century, with the construction of the South-Directed Corridor for China-Singapore interconnection as an important carrier. And opportunity to implement the strategy of opening up.

Based on location advantages, the development of the "sea going economy" is in full swing. The ancient city head of Dalang Village, Shiwan Town, Hepu County, Guangxi, is the original wharf of the "Han Dynasty Maritime Silk Road". The fleet set out from here in a vast wave and carried Chinese goods along the Maritime Silk Road to Southeast Asia, West Asia and even Africa. Over the past thousand years, ancient port has been replaced by modern port. Beihai Tieshan Port Terminal is busy with many gantry cranes operating at the same time. The running cars shuttle back and forth between the docks and yards. From here, commodities from Australia, Panama and other countries and regions are continuously transported to the southwest, South China and other places. 报错
