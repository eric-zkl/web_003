Excellent results greet the party's 19th National Congress.

In the land of Bagui in April, the spring breeze and the hustle and bustle are all colorful. From April 19 to 21, Xi Jinping, accompanied by Peng Qinghua, secretary of the Party Committee of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and Chen Wu, Chairman of the Autonomous Region Government, came to Beihai, Nanning and other places to go deep into ports, enterprises, key projects, innovation demonstration bases and cultural units. Investigate the economic and social development, and understand the suggestions and expectations of grassroots cadres and the masses on the party's 19th National Congress.

Xi Jinping pointed out that to maintain sustained and healthy economic development, we must work hard to promote industrial optimization and upgrading, work hard to change the development mode, work hard to improve innovation capabilities, and work hard to deepen reform and opening up.

Nanning

Looking back at the memories of the old Nanning - Nanning is the capital of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, the largest city in Guangxi, the core city of the Beibu Gulf Economic Zone, located in the south of central Guangxi. It is the hometown of red beans and a long-established ancient city of Bianque. Cultural accumulation, the ancient name of Zhangzhou.

Liuzhou

Liuzhou

The Liujiang Bridge is the first road bridge across the Liujiang River. It is the first bridge in Liuzhou. It connects the most densely populated areas on both sides of Liuzhou. From December 26, 1968, the Liujiang Bridge has been in operation for nearly 50 years.

Yunlin

Yunlin

In the memory, the old station building of Yulin Railway Station was delivered on the New Year's Day in 1956, and the Yulin people walked through the entire 60 years of ups and downs. Yulin, known as Yulin Prefecture, is a cultural ancient city with a history of more than 2,000 years. Known as the "Lingnan Meiyu, Shengjingrulin", Yulin has been known as the "Lingnan Metropolis" since ancient times, and is surrounded by the Beibu Gulf City Group.

Guilin

Guilin

In 1978, Guilin did not have a Shanghai road to establish an interchange. Guilin is a prefecture-level city under the jurisdiction of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, referred to as Gui. It is located in the northeast of Guangxi, in the forefront of the ASEAN Free Trade Zone, bordering Hunan and Guizhou in the north, Liuzhou in the southwest, and Hezhou in the east. It is located in the southwest of the Nanling Mountains and in Guilin. - The central part of the north end of the Yangshuo karst basin is a mountainous hilly area and a typical karst karst landform.

Beihai

Beihai

Zhuhai Road, Beihai City, “Beihai Old Street” was built at the end of the 19th century and is 1.44 kilometers long. The shops are row upon row and used to be the most prosperous commercial district in Beihai. After a hundred years of vicissitudes of life, the buildings along the street are getting old and aging, and many shops have become residential houses. However, the Chinese-Western architecture is still intact and has been hailed by architects as the “Modern Architecture Yearbook”.
