Carrying out reforms to the end

  • Building a new transportation strategy

    In the next five years, Guangxi will closely follow the country's main line of building a “transportation power” and strive to build various transportation platforms that carry the development strategy of the new era of the country and the autonomous region. In 2018, Guangxi Transportation Construction will strive to complete investment of more than 100 billion yuan, and roads, railways, waterways and civil aviation will be built in succession.

  • traffic

    ①Sino-Singapore interconnection and south-facing channel construction platform.

    ②Rural revitalization strategy platform.

    ③Integrated transportation integration development platform.

    ④Military and civilian traffic integration development platform.

    ⑤A platform for the development of new areas of transportation and new economy.

  • Create a Guangxi brand

    In order to promote the transformation of “Guangxi products” to “Guangxi brand”, we will focus on creating “one city, one product”, with Nanning Laoyou powder, Liuzhou snail powder, Guilin rice flour, Qinzhou Yuxing pottery as the “one city, one product” key support industry, and upgrade Featured brand development. Study and formulate incentives to accelerate the development of distinctive industry brands.

  • culture

    Guangxi actively participated in the construction of the “Belt and Road” and built a new pattern of “Nanxiang, Beilian, Dongrong and Xihe” in all directions, and actively promoted the Chinese culture to go out. Guangxi People’s Broadcasting Station has carried out extensive exchanges and cooperation with the Myanmar National Radio and Television Station. The launch of "Chinese Animation" opened another window for the people of Myanmar to understand China, and promoted Chinese culture to go out and build new brands.

  • Strengthening the construction of ecological environment

    In order to resolutely win the battle against pollution prevention and strengthen the infrastructure construction of ecological environment protection in our district, a few days ago, the office of the autonomous regional government issued the "Three-year Operation Plan for Guangxi Ecological Environment Protection Infrastructure Construction (2018-2020)", which further improved the whole District urban and rural living environment.

  • Ecology

    In-depth study and implementation of Xi Jinping's ecological civilization thoughts, implementation of the spirit of the National Ecological Environmental Protection Conference, analysis of the new situation and new tasks facing the ecological civilization construction in our district, demanding the determination of the strong men to break the wrist, the courage to fight the battle, the hard work of attacking the city, and resolutely fight We will fight well in the prevention and control of pollution, vigorously promote the construction of ecological civilization and ecological environment protection, and unswervingly follow the green development path with Guangxi characteristics.
