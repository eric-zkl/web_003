40 years of reform and development of the Communist Party of China

Don’t forget your heart and let the youth gather

This is the contest of “Learning the Report of the 19th National Congress of the Eight-Year Youth League”. Young people are the future of the motherland and the heirs of the nation. They will determine the fate of the motherland. Looking back 40 years ago, the older generation of revolutionaries led the people to rise from the predicament with great fearless spirit and created a historical monument to the rejuvenation. Today, 40 years later, China's development faces unprecedented opportunities and faces unprecedented challenges. Today, when we look back on the unforgettable years of 40 years, we must fully understand the great significance and great achievements of reform and opening up, and learn from the passion of reform and the power of development. We must strengthen our own ideas and shoulder the heavy responsibilities of the times. The strength of youth social responsibility will be related to the process of building a well-off society in an all-round way. It is related to whether or to what extent we can shoulder the historical mission of realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.

This is the contest of “Learning the Report of the 19th National Congress of the Eight-Year Youth League”.

The theme of the speech of the coach of the Young Pioneers of Wang Dong Primary School, Qiu Chunlande, is "Small Buttons and Big Future." She tells the story of herself and the students, and plays the role of deducting the first button of life for children. It motivates young teachers to deduct the key to the initial heart and mission of education and set a big future.

The theme of the speech of Mo Zikai, the head of the technical department of the People's Procuratorate of Heshan City, is "Reading You, Knowing the Responsibility in the Shoulder". She tells the story of integrating love into work and making the law warm, and explains the spirit of youth inspectors who have love and love at work.

The theme of the speech by Li Xuming, a long-term graduate student of the Chinese People's Public Security University, is "The New Era of Life, Dedication is the Time." He said that he was born in the new era and was responsible for the new era. He called on the young and good children to be willing to work in the Quartet and realize the great rejuvenation dream of the Chinese nation in the relay struggle.

The theme of the speech by Wang Sheng, a policeman from the Chengbei Police Station of the Quanzhou County Public Security Bureau, is "Choice." He is telling the story of a group of public security police guardians behind the peaceful Guilin. Praise the young police for choosing to face the dangerous black and evil forces and take up the spirit and mission of serving the people, loving the people and protecting the people.

Chen Yuru’s speech at the Xinggang Town Government of Beihai City is entitled “Youth assumes the mission and helps the poor to attack the hardship”. She shared a touching story of a young “first secretary” leading the villagers to get rich, explaining to the youth that doing small things is the foundation of doing big things.

Talking about the fruits of development

Recently, the Office of the Party Committee of the Autonomous Region held a symposium on retirees “seeing 60 years of development in Guangxi, new achievements in reform and opening up”, and more than 50 retired veteran comrades attended the forum. Shao Qixian, deputy inspector of the Office of the Party Committee of the Autonomous Region, presided over the meeting.

At the symposium, veteran comrades combined their own experiences, from the perspectives of people's livelihood improvement, economic development, transportation construction, poverty alleviation, foreign exchanges, strict governance of the party, anti-corruption and honesty, etc., and detailed 40 years of reform and opening up and 60 years since the establishment of the autonomous region. Great changes have taken place in all aspects of clothing, food, housing, transportation and education, health care, and health. Everyone agrees that the Chinese people’s pockets are getting more and more drums, their lives are getting better and better, their travels are getting more and more convenient, their waists are getting harder and harder, and the Chinese nation has ushered in a great leap from standing up, getting rich and strengthening, from being closed and old. To reform and opening up, from poverty alleviation to prosperity and prosperity, from the edge of the world to the center of the world stage, from the search for the Chinese plan... The road to socialism with Chinese characteristics is self-confident, theoretically confident, institutionally confident, and culturally confident. Resolutely, the political ecology of the party’s internal atmosphere is gradually manifested, and the sense of gain, happiness and satisfaction of the masses have significantly improved.
